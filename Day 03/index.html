3 Keys to Accelerated Learning

Three techniques you can use to learn faster and more effectively.

One key to accelerated learning is active recall. Actively making the effort to produce the information drastically increases retention.
Another key is spaced repetition. Recalling the information in increasingly spaced-out intervals of time.
One more key to accelerated learning is to keep a beginner's mind. This means going into new subjects with an open mind, without old misconceptions.
